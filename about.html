Competency Based Curriculum, Kenya

The CBC curriculum has 5 levels in total. Teachers are expected to keep their professional documents current. Th documents are Schemes of work, Lesson Plans and Assessment Rubrics. The information that is required to fill out these documents is contained in the Curriculum Designs from the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Developement.

This website has been created to digitally store the curriculum designs and professional documents in a format that will help make retrieval of the information therein easier for the teachers.

This is a demo and will continue to be populated. Click on the button to generate the professional documents per strand.
